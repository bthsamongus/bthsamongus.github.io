Info

At BTHS Among Us, students at Brooklyn Tech bring their gamer instinct, pursuasion skills, and mischeviousness to the table. May the craftiest, luckiest, sneakiest crewmates and impostors emerge victorious in a galaxy far, far away! Without the need of knowing anyone in our club, come find your destined friends and matches in our community of social detectives - and impostors!

We meet biweekly on Tuesdays and Thursdays on Zoom! 3:30 - 4:30 PM on Tuesdays. 2:30 - 3:30 PM on Thursdays. Members are only required to join one meeting on every week that we meet for full credit! If you are really bored, you are totally allowed to attend both meetings on the same week - but you won't get extra credit!
