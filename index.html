we are bths among us

This is where students at Brooklyn Tech can interact with other students through the online game Among Us! Everyone gets to make new friends and find new people to play with outside of school hours.

We meet on Tuesdays 3:30 - 4:30 PM and Thursdays 2:30 -3:30 PM on Zoom every other week. Members are only required to attend at least one meeting every week we meet for full attendance credit for that week. They are allowed to attend both if they want, but there won't be any extra credit.
